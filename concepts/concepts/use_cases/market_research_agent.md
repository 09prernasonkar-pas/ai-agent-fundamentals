# Market Research Agent

## Problem
Product teams spend significant time manually researching competitors and market trends.

## Agent Solution
An AI agent that:
1. Searches competitor information
2. Extracts features and pricing
3. Identifies patterns
4. Produces a structured summary

## Benefits
- Faster insights
- Reduced manual effort
- Consistent analysis

## Risks
- Outdated sources
- Bias in data selection

## Metrics
- Accuracy of insights
- Time saved
- Adoption by teams
